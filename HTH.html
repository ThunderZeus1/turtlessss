WELCOME TO HIKKADUWA TURTLE HATCHERY

One of the area's well-known turtle conservation initiatives is the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery. Its main objective is to save and care for sea turtle eggs and hatchlings before releasing them into the water when they are old enough. The several types of sea turtles may be seen by visitors, who can also learn about their life cycles, dangers they encounter, and conservation activities. The hatchery also gives visitors the chance to take part in the marine release of young turtles.

Experience includes

  • Release Of Baby Turtles
  • Private Guide
  • Water Bottles
  • Cold Towels
